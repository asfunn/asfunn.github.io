How-to-essay: how to make a bookmark

Materials

  • Paper
  • Coloring tool
  • Scissors
  • Ruler
  • pencil
  • Tape(optional)

Instrutions

  1. Take the ruler, take your paper
    and your pencil, then draw a vertical rectangle(just make a
    rectangle that goes
    up and down). Then take your
    coloring tool and color it.
  2. And then take your scissors and cut it out. Then you can
    put your tape on top of your drawing.

Now enjoy your very own book mark!
