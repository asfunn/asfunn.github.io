All about bunnies

What are bunnies

Bunnies are furry creatures that are peaceful and
calm creatures. They have long ears and puffy tails,
and they move their nose up and down in an adorable way.
They eat the most orange vegetables in our world, and they reproduce
more than any human ever has.

where bunnies live

bunnies can be found everywhere, except of course Antarctica.
